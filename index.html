⛽
Nearby petrol pumps
Authorised PUC vendors · on-the-spot
Most petrol pumps have an authorised PUC vendor on-site who issues your Pollution Under Control Certificate in a couple of minutes — no appointment needed.
How to get it
1
Drive to any nearby petrol pump or authorised emission-testing centre — look for the green “Pollution Check / PUC” board.
2
Keep your RC handy and take the vehicle — the tailpipe emission test is done on the spot.
3
The vendor runs the test (~2–3 min) and prints your PUCC immediately.
4
Pay the nominal fee — usually ₹60–₹110 (varies by fuel type & state).
✅ Your PUCC auto-syncs to Vahan. Once you have it, come back and upload it here. PUC counters are usually open 8 AM – 10 PM.
